# Pelican on a bicycle with city behind, postcard

Draw an SVG of a pelican riding a bicycle. The pelican should be
recognizable as a pelican (long beak, throat pouch, large body). The
bicycle should have two wheels of similar size, a frame, and handlebars.
The composition should suggest the pelican is actually pedaling — feet
near the pedals, posture leaning slightly forward.

There should be a visible city skyline behind the pelican with realistic buildings.

Style is up to you. Flat colors, line art, or a mix all fine. Keep it on
a clean background. The SVG must parse as XML and render in any modern browser.

The whole composition should feel like a postcard. You can add text to improve postcard feel.

## Visual Details
- **Dimensions**: 800x600 pixels.
- **Background**: A warm sky gradient (light blue to soft yellow) to suggest a sunny day.
- **City Skyline**: A row of varied building silhouettes in the background, slightly faded to create depth.
- **Bicycle**: A classic road bike style with thin tires and drop handlebars.
- **Pelican**: White or light grey body, large orange/yellow beak and throat pouch. The pelican should be seated on the saddle, with wings extended forward to grip the handlebars, and feet on the pedals.
- **Postcard Elements**: A white border around the SVG. Text at the bottom reading "Greetings from Pelican City" in a playful, retro font.
