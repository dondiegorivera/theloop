# The Trip

Write a scene where two people are packing for a trip neither of them actually
wants to take.

Hard constraints:
- Approximately 200 words.
- No dialogue tags beyond he/she/they or action beats.
- No internal monologue, exposition, or direct emotional labels.

Quality rubric:
- Convey tension entirely through objects, movement, and subtext.
- Every sentence should add pressure, specificity, or implication.
- The ending image should reveal a shift without explaining it.
